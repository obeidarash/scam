How does the site work?

As soon as you charge up your account, our robots will start investing your money in different cryptocurrencies. The same thing happens to the three people who registered with your token. Where and how much to invest depends entirely on the artificial intelligence of robots. Finally, $100 of profit will be transfer to your wallet id.

How to earn 100 Dollars in less than 24 hours?

  1. Register using a Accesses Token (if you don't have any Token, you can get one from a friend, otherwise click here)
  2. Transfer 100 Theater to our wallet id (wallet number) and register it through the Deposit menu in you account (Wait for confirmation)
  3. After Deposit Conformation, three tokens will be issued to you, share the tokens with your friends to register with it.
  4. All three users must charge their deposit just like you
  5. After confirming the deposit of all three users, Withdraw request will be opened for you, enter your wallet address and submit the form. 200 Theater will be transfer into your wallet in less than a few hours. Finish!

p.s: your friends can do the same to earn 100 Theater
